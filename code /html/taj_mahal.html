Agra Fort

Also known as Lal Qila,the Agra Fort is a UNESCO world heritage site. It is situated at a distance of about 2.5 km northwest of the famous Taj Mahal.

Fatehpur Sikri

A city, predominantly made of red sand stone, Fatehpur Sikri was once the capital of Mughal Emperor Akbar. a famous tourist attraction.

Jama Masjid

Jama Masjid in Agra is opposite to the Agra Fort and overlooking the Agra Fort Railway Station. The Jama Masjid is also popularly known as the Jami Masjid or "Friday Mosque".
